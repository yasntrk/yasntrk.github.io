Privacy Policy

A dice roguelike by Yasin Türk.

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the DICO mobile game ("the Game", "we", "our") handles information. DICO is a single-player game. We do not run our own servers and we do not directly collect, store, or sell your personal information. Your game progress is stored locally on your device. Some limited data is processed by the third-party Google services described below when you use certain optional features.

Developer contact: ysntrktr@gmail.com

01 Information the Game processes

DICO itself asks for no name, email, phone number, or location. The only information involved is handled by the optional Google services the Game integrates:

Advertising — Google AdMob

DICO offers optional rewarded video ads (for example, to revive a run or earn cosmetic currency). When an ad is shown, Google AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads, in line with Google's advertising policies. You are never required to watch an ad to play.

Cloud save & achievements — Google Play Games

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, your Play Games account identifier is used to back up your progress and record achievements to your Google account. Signing in is optional; the Game is fully playable without it.

In-app purchases — Google Play Billing

DICO offers optional purchases of cosmetic items and in-game currency. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play. We never receive or store your payment card or billing details — Google handles all payment information.

02 Data stored on your device

Your progress, unlocked cosmetics, currency, and settings are saved locally on your device. If you opt into Google Play Games sign-in, a copy of this progress may also be backed up to your Google account (Saved Games) so you can restore it. Uninstalling the Game removes the local data from your device.

03 The advertising identifier

The advertising ID is used only by Google AdMob to show and measure ads. You can reset it or opt out of personalized ads at any time from your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads on Android).

04 Children

DICO is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service, please contact us and we will help address it.

05 Data sharing & third parties

We do not sell your data. The Game relies on the Google services listed above, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob & ad data
  • Google Play Games

06 Your choices

  • Play without signing in to Google Play Games.
  • Skip ads and in-app purchases — both are optional.
  • Reset or limit your advertising ID in device settings.
  • Uninstall the Game to remove locally stored data.

07 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Game evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

08 Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach the developer at ysntrktr@gmail.com.
